---

OB2 is an open-source, self-hosted retrieval-augmented generation platform. Upload documents in any format, ask questions of a local LLM, and get grounded answers with clickable source citations — all running on your own hardware.

Key capabilities: multi-format ingestion (PDFs with OCR, audio, Office documents, URLs), multi-user with per-domain access control, Open WebUI chat surface with SSO, signed citation links that link back to original files, Python and Rust retrieval sidecars, full Docker deployment.

Apache-2.0 licensed. Documentation in the `docs/` directory.
